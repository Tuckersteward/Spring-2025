About Me

Hi, I’m Cabot Steward, a passionate photographer with a love for capturing unique and breathtaking moments. I’ve been honing my craft since I was 10 years old, diving into the worlds of sports, landscape, and adventure photography.

My journey began with a simple camera and a curiosity for the outdoors. Over the years, I’ve turned my passion into a profession, photographing everything from high-octane sporting events to serene landscapes and daring adventures.

My goal is to tell a story through every image, sharing the emotion and beauty of each moment. When I’m not behind the lens, you can find me exploring new trails, climbing mountains, or chasing the perfect sunset.
